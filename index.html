    Wedding Dress Shopping:
    What To Expect at Your Appointment

    by Good Oomph Works

You’ve chosen a wedding venue, planned your guest list and maybe even your honeymoon. Now it’s
time to find perhaps the most important detail: your wedding dress. Shopping for a bridal gown can
be overwhelming, but The Wedding Shoppe wants to make it easier for all of our brides! Here are
some of our best tips for wedding dress shopping and what to expect during your wedding dress
appointment.

Be Prepared

Real TWS Bride Stephanie O’Connell
Photo Courtesy of Dayna Mae Photography

Prior to wedding dress shopping, the bride should know a few key things about her wedding. Details
such as your budget, the wedding date, and venue are important to consider while shopping. We also
recommend knowing who you want to bring with you, whether that’s friends, family or both. Make a
list of all the people who are special to you and be sure to let them know your shopping date ahead of
time. However, don’t bring too many as too many opinions can often be overwhelming for the bride-to-be.

Wedding Dress Shopping

Now comes the fun part – when you finally get to shop for a gown! At this point, the bride and stylist
will look through the available dresses. They usually start with about five options to get a feel for the
brides’ preferences. After that, the stylist will know what direction to go in and pull the appropriate
dresses going forward. Don’t be afraid if you have to try on several different ones – sometimes your
perfect dress is the last one you find!

Bring Your Friends

Real TWS Bride Brooke MacGregor
Photo Courtesy of Molly Grunewald

Oftentimes when brides arrive at The Wedding Shoppe, they aren’t expecting to find their dress – but
they should be! Instead of taking several trips to several different bridal stores in search of the right
dress, we’ve found that brides will most likely find their perfect match the first time. When a bride has
her “moment” with the lucky dress, it’s very special and should be cherished. We believe it’s just not
the same if she’s missing even one of her special people, and that “moment” is hard to re-create.
That’s why it’s so important to have everyone with you – family, friends, and anyone else you want to
share that moment with! Are you ready to find your dream wedding dress? The Wedding Shoppe would love to help – book an appointment with our bridal specialists today for the wedding dress shopping experience of your dreams! You also don’t want to forget your bridesmaids!
